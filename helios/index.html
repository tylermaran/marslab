Helios OBC
  • Helios is a small, low-power, high performance computer designed for use on satellite.
  • The Helios OBC is capable of operating in harsh radiation and temperature environments, making it ideal for space applications.
  • Helios comes equipped with an array of sensors including accelerometer, orientation, GPS, and magnetic field detection.
  • Capable of running standard Linux kernal, as well as proprietary SatCom SDK.
Specifications
  • Broadcom BCM2711, Quad core Cortex-A72 (ARM v8) 64-bit SoC @ 1.5GHz
  • 1GB, 2GB, 4GB or 8GB LPDDR4-3200 SDRAM (depending on model)
  • 2.4 GHz and 5.0 GHz IEEE 802.11ac wireless, Bluetooth 5.0, BLE Gigabit Ethernet
  • H.265 (4kp60 decode), H264 (1080p60 decode, 1080p30 encode)
